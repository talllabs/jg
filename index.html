About Me

My name is Jaclyn Gaffaney, though you can call me Jackie. I am a PhD trained evaluator, researcher, practitioner, and professor with subject matter expertise in wellbeing science, positive psychology, organizational psychology, applied research, and evaluation. I am based in Seattle and find joy in bridging the research-practice gap.

Services Offered

I offer evaluation, research, and practitioner services. My work is tailored to your project's needs, often using a mixed-methods approach.

Evaluation Services

I help clients measure their work to assess effectiveness and impact, and to discover areas for improvement.

Academic & Applied Research Services

I conduct research for both applied and academic purposes, with an expertise in scale development and validation.

Practitioner Services - Program Design & Trainings

I design, implement, and manage programs, initiatives, and interventions. I also conduct workshops and trainings.

Research and Evaluation Skills

  • Designs & Methods: mixed methods, longitudinal, experimental, survey & scale development, needs assessment, and more.
  • Analyses: regression, ANOVA, factor analysis, thematic analysis, and more.
  • Reporting and Recommendations: report creation, data walks, and using design thinking to translate findings into action.

Approaches

  • Bridging the Research-Practice Gap
  • Systems-Informed and Sustainable
  • Contextually and Culturally Responsive
  • Access and Inclusion
  • Wellbeing and Strengths-Based
  • Evidence-Informed and Experimental
